How to order on Foodpanda App

To follow this step and get the best offer for you

Select Category:

Once you've selected a category, the app will display a list of restaurants that offer that type of cuisine or food.

Browse Restaurants:

Browse through the list of available restaurants in your area. You can filter by cuisine, rating, or other preferences.

Select a item Category:

Click or tap on the category that interests you. You can filter by cuisine, rating, or other preferences.

Select Your Food:

Choose the restaurant you want to order from and select items from their menu. You can usually view details and prices for each dish.

Add Items to Your Cart:

Select the items you want to order from the restaurant's menu, add them to your cart, and specify any customizations or quantity adjustments.

Review Your Order:

Double-check your order in the cart to ensure it's correct. You can also apply any available promo codes or discounts.

Provide Delivery Instructions and Payment Method:

Follow the previous steps as outlined in the previous response to provide delivery instructions, select your payment method, and place your order.

Place Your Order:

After confirming all the details, place your order. You'll receive an order confirmation with an estimated delivery time.

Receive Your Food:

Once your food is delivered, pay the delivery driver (if you selected cash on delivery) and enjoy your meal.

FAQ's

Frequently asked questions

Foodpanda is an online food delivery and food ordering platform that allows you to order food from local restaurants and have it delivered to your doorstep.

You can download the Foodpanda app from your device's app store, such as the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

Foodpanda's availability depends on your location. You can check if the service is available in your area by entering your address in the app or on their website.

To place an order, open the app, enter your location, browse restaurants, select items from their menus, and add them to your cart. Once you've made your selections, proceed to checkout, choose your payment method, and confirm your order.

Many food delivery apps, including Foodpanda, offer real-time order tracking. You can see the status of your order and the estimated delivery time.

Delivery fees vary depending on the restaurant and your location. Some restaurants may offer free delivery for certain order amounts.

Foodpanda usually has customer support available through the app or website. You can contact them to resolve any issues with your order or delivery.
